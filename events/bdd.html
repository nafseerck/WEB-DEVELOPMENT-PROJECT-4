ARKADIKO

ARKADIKO is a Bridge Design event organized by Civil Engineering Department as a part of the South Indian Techno-Cultural fest QUASSO LIBERUM 5 (2k16) in which the participants are required to construct a bridge of 1m span using pop sticks. The main objective is to attain the load carrying capacity. Ignite your ideas to strengthen your Bridge…!!

Important Dates

Last date to register through phone or e-mail : 07 March 2016 ARKADIKO - QL2k16 : 09 March 2016

SPECIFICATION

• Span should be exactly 1 meter.
• Only 50% overlap is allowed.
• Cuttings are not allowed.

MATERIALS PROVIDED

• 500 Pop sticks.
• Minimum adhesive is provided (No extra will be provided)

Rules

1.The contest is open for students of Civil Engineering Stream only.
2.Team should be of six members.
3.External participants can register through mail or contact respective event coordinator.
4.The judgment is based on the load carrying capacity of the bridge.
5.All the participants are expected to abide by the rules and regulations set by the institution for this event.
6.The judge’s decision will be final.
7.Please feel free to clarify your valuable doubts and we are eager to guide you.
