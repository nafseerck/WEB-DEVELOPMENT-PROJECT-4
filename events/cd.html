CIRCUIT DESIGNING

This circuit is interesting because it has inclines and declines. Not just up, but down as well.

Rules

1) Participant can contest as a team of 2.
2) If more than 10 participants have registered there will be 2 rounds.
3) First round will be elimination round with objective type questions from field of electronics.
4) Second round will be circuit designing round in which actual application has to be made.
5) Both rounds will be open in nature, means you will be provided with question sheets/components and you need to submit them back within the specified time limit.
6) Power supply and other testing devices like multimeter, CRO (if needed) and other tools will be made available in the shared hall.
7) The circuit must be designed using the components provided. You may not use any other component/material, however in case something get damaged you may request the organiser to replace the components/ parts.
8) Students should draw circuit diagram of the application depicting all the principles and theories involved in the circuit as well as the working of the application on a chart sheet.
