ELYSIUM ARCHITECTO

ELYSIUM ARCHITECTO is a Town Planning event organized by Civil Engineering Department as a part of the South Indian Techno-Cultural fest QUASSO LIBERUM 5 (2k16) in which the participants are required to plan and design a township. The main objective is to analyse the participant’s skill in planning and designing a SELF-SUSTAINED TOWNSHIP, meeting all specified requirements. Hey Town Planner, Excited to plan your future..??

IMPORTANT DATES

DURATION

3 Hours

Rules

1.The contest is open for students of Civil Engineering Stream only.
2.Team should consist of a maximum of three members only.
3.External participants can register through mail or contact respective event coordinator.
4.Scientific calculators are allowed.
5.Use of colour pens/sketch pens are permitted.
6.The judgment is based on the functional utility as well as aesthetics involved in the design.
7.The participants are not allowed to use mobile phone or other electronic instruments.
8.All the participants are expected to abide by the rules and regulations set by the institution for this event.
9.Decision of QL team will be final.

SPECIFICATION

1.All the requirements specified should be clearly met within the design.
2.The design should be in accordance with the theme of the competition.

MATERIALS PROVIDED

1.A2 Drawing Sheet.
2.Participants can bring any drawing equipments they wish to use.
