QUAESITOR

QUAESITOR is a technical paper presentation event organized by Civil Engineering Department as a part of the South Indian Techno-Cultural fest QUASSO LIBERUM 5 (2k16) in which the participants are required to present a technical paper related to civil engineering field. It is a great platform to express and share your ideas. An Idea can grab a Prize…!!

Important Dates

Last date to send the abstract     : 04 March 2016
Last date to send the full paper  : 07 March 2016
QUAESITOR - QL2K16             : 09 March 2016

Rules

1.The contest is open for students of Civil Engineering Stream only.
2.There can be a maximum of two members in a team.
3.The last date of abstract submission in pdf format is 04th March 2016.
4.Topic should be related to Civil Engineering stream only.
5.The abstract should include clear idea about the paper, name of authors, phone number, e-mail id, college, semester etc.
6.The abstracts should be send to qlcivilcev@gmail.com
7.The selected participants will be informed individually (by e- mail and phone).
8.The selected participants should send the full paper to qlcivilcev@gmail.com on or before 7th March 2016, 11:00 AM.
9.The final paper should be in IEEE standard paper format (in pdf format).
10.15 minutes time is allotted to each team of which 10 minutes is for paper presentation and 5 minutes for questionnaire and discussion.
11.The judgment is based on the presentation as well as the technical worth of the paper.
12.All the participants are expected to abide by the rules and regulations set by the institution for this event.
13.Decision of QL team will be final.
